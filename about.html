Who we are — PetCart

PetCart is an online pet store built for families across Sri Lanka who want trusted food, toys, and care products without the guesswork. We focus on clear product information, fair pricing in LKR, and reliable delivery so you spend less time shopping and more time with your pets.

Our mission

To make high-quality pet nutrition and accessories easy to find, easy to order, and backed by support when you need it — whether you shop for a playful pup, a curious cat, or smaller companions.

Why shop with us

We curate products with your pet’s health and safety in mind, keep our catalog simple to navigate, and work continuously to improve checkout, delivery updates, and customer care.
